Who Are We

Little Bunnies Circle is a marketplace and a support community for families both just starting with their journey as parents and those who already have kids who are all grown-up. We support the circular sustainaibility movement by encouraging our bunny mums and pops to place their dusty but still usable baby items into our marketplace directory. Not only are we contributing to save the environment but we save cash as well.
