Frequently Asked Questions

We are a marketplace that supports circular sustainaibility, a new consumer movement that promotes the use of products to its highest value to lessen the environmental impact involved in product production (resources, carbon emissions, etc). How? We reward our members whenever they decide to swap out their still usable items. Not only are you saving cash but you're also taking part to lessen waste.

We deliver nationwide. Fees and charges depends on your location.

Get rewarded when you decide to swap out your items (subject to evaluation by our trusty QC team) with a new one from our directory.
